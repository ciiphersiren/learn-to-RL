Exploration (ε-greedy)

ε = 1.00 — probability of random action
High ε = exploring. Low ε = exploiting what it learned.

What's Happening Now

The agent starts knowing nothing. It will take random actions and slowly build a Q-table — a lookup of "how good is action A in state S?"

Q-Learning Formula

Q(s,a) ← Q(s,a) + α[r + γ·max Q(s') − Q(s,a)]

α = learning rate (how fast to update)
γ = discount (value future rewards)
r = reward just received
s' = next state after action
